### Mean

The "average" number; found by adding all data points and dividing by the number of data points.

Example:

dataset: `4, 1, 7`, mean = `(4 + 1 + 7) / 3` = `4`

```math {.text-center}
\text{mean}=\dfrac{\sum{x_i}}{n}
```
